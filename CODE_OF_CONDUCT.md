# Code of Conduct

OdinSync Platform is intended to be a respectful, collaborative project.

## Our Standards

- Be kind, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still holding a high bar for quality and safety.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and inflammatory behavior.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers.
Reports will be reviewed with care and handled as confidentially as possible.
