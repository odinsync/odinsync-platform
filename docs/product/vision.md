# Product Vision

## Product Name

OdinSync

## Tagline

Synchronize. Automate. Grow.

## Vision

Build an intelligent Business Operating System for SMEs.

## Mission

Help small and medium businesses manage customers, products, inventory, orders, invoices, payments, and future AI insights from one platform.

## Problem

SMEs often use disconnected tools such as Excel, WhatsApp, email, Tally, inventory software, and payroll software.

This causes:
- Duplicate data
- Manual work
- Delayed reporting
- Poor visibility
- Human errors

## Solution

OdinSync connects business workflows into one system.

Customer → Sales Order → Inventory Reservation → Invoice → Payment → Dashboard → AI Insights

## MVP Scope

Included:
- Authentication
- Organization
- Customers
- Products
- Inventory
- Sales Orders
- Invoices
- Payments

Not included in MVP:
- AI
- Kafka
- Kubernetes
- Payroll
- Manufacturing
