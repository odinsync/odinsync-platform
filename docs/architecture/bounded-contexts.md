# Bounded Contexts

## Identity & Access

Owns:
- Tenant
- User
- Role
- Permission
- Authentication

## Organization

Owns:
- Organization
- Branch
- Business settings

## CRM

Owns:
- Customer
- Contact
- Customer notes

## Catalog

Owns:
- Product
- Category
- Brand
- Pricing

## Inventory

Owns:
- Warehouse
- Stock
- Stock reservation
- Stock movement

## Sales

Owns:
- Sales order
- Sales order item
- Order status

## Finance

Owns:
- Invoice
- Payment
- Tax
- Ledger entries

## Notification

Owns:
- Email
- SMS
- WhatsApp messages

## Analytics

Reads business data and creates dashboards.

## AI Copilot

Future module for natural language business insights.
